New mysterious beaver found in ice!

The Insider trader

Two weeks ago, researchers in Alaska came across an ice cave in some of the most remote areas of the world. With the guidance of legendary scientist Kevin Bacon, they were able to find a large ice wall with a small creature in it. After excavation had ended, they found it resembled a beaver, but with a shell on its back. It is assumed that this creature existed back when Alaska was more swampy, as it is unlikely it would survive cold temperatures.
