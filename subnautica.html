Subnautica 2 releases soon.

The Insider trader

The current number one wishlisted game on Steam is Subnautica 2. Subnautica is an ocean-based survival/horror game set on an alien planet. It has suffered from major legal setbacks after the publisher (Krafton Games) fired three head directors trying to stop the team from getting a 250 million dollar bonus. Unknown Worlds has won the court case, and the game is planned to release May 14th.
